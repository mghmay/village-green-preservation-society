Help us keep Little Whinging green.

Join our vibrant community for the preservation of Little Whinging Green. Keep calm and plant trees. We are a free group sponsored by Mrs. Baker's all meat pies. Their Little Whinging's finest!

Find out what's happening on Little Whinging Green this month.

Mrs. Lovelace's fantastic bake sale.

Join us once again for this year's bake sale. You're welcome to bring along your own baked goods or just peruse the offerings at your leasure. All proceeds go to fund the rebuilding of the church steeple. Free entry. Saturday, 5th March. Starts at around 8am.

The all new and improved Little Whinging car boot sale

Come down and see what various odds and assortments the citizens of Little Whinging have for sale. You can look around or even decide to sell something if you'd like!Free entry but £15 charge to set up shop (Free to set up for members). Sunday, 20th March. Starts at around 6am.

Mr. and Mrs. Baker's famous barbecue cook out.

Come one come all to the Baker's famous barbecue cook out. Bring your friends and family to eat great meat and have a good old sing song on the Village Green. We'll have a wonderful time!Free for members or £5 entry fee for non-members. Saturday, 26th March. Starts at around 5pm.
